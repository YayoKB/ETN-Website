Landscaping

Cost: R1500

This learnership teaches how to provide landscaping services for new and established gardens.

What you will learn:

  • Indigenous and exotic plants and trees
  • Fixed structures (fountains, statues, benches, tables, built-in braai)
  • Balancing of plant and trees in a garden
  • Aesthetics of plant shapes and colours
  • Garden Layout
